# BWAI Stock Research

BWAI (Buy With AI) is an AI-powered stock research assistant that helps users quickly understand whether a stock is worth deeper research.

## Overview
BWAI turns fragmented market information into a simple, structured summary. Users can enter a stock ticker such as AAPL, NVDA, or TSLA and receive a clear explanation of the current signal, key trends, risks, and the main bull-versus-bear considerations.

## Project Goals
- Make stock research faster and easier to understand
- Help retail investors focus on meaningful signals instead of market noise
- Provide a simple experience for web and mobile users

- AI-generated research summaries
- Bull vs bear analysis

- Cached results and deployment
